About

I'm Zelaf Ary and I enjoy web development and photography. I'm a bit experienced in photography but there's always more to learn! I'm newer to web development but I'm quite fond of it so far.
I started with photography when I got an old camera from my teacher. It was a manual analog camera, from that I learned a lot about camera settings and also got photography tips from my art teacher. Since then I've gone digital and try to take the best photos I can even with my limited hardware.

On the other hand, I started web development when I got my Raspberry Pi B+ one January after christmas. I made text websites that looked like something from the mid 90s. It was nothing impressive, since then I've gotten better and found better resources from the internet.

If you want to use my photos or other projects in your project (Blog, YouTube, Article, Website etc.) please contact me below and we can discuss it. Or you can just say hi :)
